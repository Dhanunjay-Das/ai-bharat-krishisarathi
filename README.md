## TL;DR for Judges
KrishiSarathi is a voice-first AI assistant for Indian farmers that:
- Works across smartphones and feature phones
- Supports regional languages (Hindi-first MVP)
- Diagnoses crop diseases with confidence scores
- Explains AI recommendations in simple language
- Escalates low-confidence cases to human experts

Built for Bharat. Designed for scale.

## Problem Statement
Indian farmers face language barriers, digital divide, and lack of
timely, reliable agricultural guidance, leading to crop loss and income reduction.

## Solution Overview
KrishiSarathi provides voice-based agricultural assistance through:
- Disease diagnosis
- Government scheme eligibility checks
- Market price intelligence
- Location-based weather alerts
